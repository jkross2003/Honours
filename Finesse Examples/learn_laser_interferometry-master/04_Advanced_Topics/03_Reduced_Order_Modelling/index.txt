<p>
This section demonstrates how to use the reduced order modelling
features in Finesse and Pykat which  allow for fast computation of
higher-order mode scattering by producing a very efficient quadrature
rule.
</p>

<p>
More details on this method can be found in the paper <a
href="http://iopscience.iop.org/article/10.1088/2040-8978/18/2/025604">"Fast
simulation of Gaussian-mode scattering for precision interferometry"
Brown, et.al. 2016</a>.
</p>
