<p>
In this directory you find introductory material to IPython/Jupyter Notebook,
the simulation software FINESSE and its Python wrapper
PyKat. IPython/Jupyter notebook is the interface between you and this
material. FINESSE is a frequency domain simulation software for laser
interferometry, specialised at gravitational wave laser
interferometry. It is actively being used for commissioning and design
of gravitational wave detectors. 
</p>
